<Heading green='The Genesis' postGreen='of the Rolex Daytona' />

<Text
  preBlack="The Rolex Daytona made its debut in 1963, initially designed for professional racing drivers. Named after the famed Daytona International Speedway in Florida, the watch was equipped with a tachymeter scale on the bezel, allowing drivers to measure average speeds over a specific distance—a crucial feature for racing.
However, it wasn't until the 1970s that the Daytona gained widespread popularity, thanks in part to a Hollywood legend—Paul Newman."
/>

<SingleImage image='article6-2.png' />

<Heading preGreen="Paul Newman's" green='Influence' />

<Text preBlack="Paul Newman, the iconic actor and racing enthusiast, played a pivotal role in elevating the status of the Rolex Daytona. In the early 1970s, Newman was often spotted wearing a Daytona with an exotic dial variant characterized by contrasting sub-dials and an Art Deco-style font. This particular design, now famously known as the 'Paul Newman' dial, added a touch of flair and uniqueness to the watch." />

<MdxImages firstImage='article6-3.png' secondImage='article6-4.png' />

<Heading green='The Legend' postGreen='of the Paul Newman Daytona' />

<Text preBlack="The legend goes that Paul Newman's wife, Joanne Woodward, gifted him the Rolex Daytona with the exotic dial as a token of love. The actor, already an accomplished racer, embraced the watch not just as a timekeeping tool but as a symbol of his passion for motorsports. This association between Paul Newman and the Daytona catapulted the watch to cult status." />

<MdxImages firstImage='article6-5.png' secondImage='article6-6.png' />

<Heading preGreen="Collector's" green='Dream' />

<Text preBlack="The Rolex Daytona Paul Newman edition has since become one of the most sought-after and valuable watches in the world of horology. Its scarcity and historical significance have turned it into a collector's dream, with auctions reaching astronomical figures. In 2017, a Rolex Daytona once owned by Paul Newman himself set a record as the most expensive wristwatch ever sold at auction." />

<MdxImages firstImage='article6-7.png' secondImage='article6-8.png' />

<Heading preGreen='Legacy and' green='Modern Interpretations' />

<Text preBlack='While the original Paul Newman Daytona watches from the 1960s and 1970s are exceedingly rare, Rolex has paid homage to this iconic design with modern interpretations. The brand released the Rolex Daytona Cosmograph with the Paul Newman-style dial, capturing the essence of the vintage models while incorporating contemporary advancements in watchmaking technology.' />

Whether you're a racing enthusiast, a watch connoisseur, or simply a lover of timeless style, the Rolex Daytona Paul Newman edition remains a captivating piece of horological history." />
